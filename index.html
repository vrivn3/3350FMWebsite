Create your own library

  • Navigate to the "Search" section at the bottom of the page.

  • Navigate to the "Song" panel.

  • Access a wide variety of songs available in the panel.

  • Find the song you want to add to your library.

  • Click on the square next to the song.

  • A checkmark will appear, indicating the song has been selected.

  • Once the song is checkmarked, it is automatically added to your personal library.

Rate and review songs

  • It can be done from your Library or, the search page for Songs, Artists and Albums.

  • Tap on the "Profile" icon in the bottom navigation bar.

  • Tap on the "Library" option on the profile screen.

  • Click on the song you want to review from your library.

  • Tap on the plus sign next to "Review" on the right.

  • Select the number of stars you want to give.

  • Write your review in the provided text field.

  • Click on "Done" to submit your review.

    • Your review will then appear in the "Reviews" section on your profile.

    Follow your friends

    • Navigate to the "Search" section at the bottom of the page.

    • Navigate to the "Users" panel.

    • Find the user you want to add to your network.

    • Click on the square next to the user.

    • A checkmark will appear, indicating the user has been selected.

    • Once the user is checkmarked, they are automatically added to your network of "following".

    Share and receive tailored recommendations

    • It can be done from your Library or the search page for Songs, Artists, and Albums.

    • Tap on the "Profile" icon in the bottom navigation bar.

    • Tap on the "Library" option on the profile screen.

    • Click on the song you want to review from your library.

    • Tap on the plus sign next to "Review" on the right.

    • Select the number of stars you want to give.

    • Write your review in the provided text field.

    • Click on "Done" to submit your review.

    Your review will then appear in the "Reviews" section on your profile.

    View charts

    Discover which songs are trending and have the highest or lowest ratings.

    • Navigate to the "Charts" section on the navigation bar.

    • There you will see the chart.

    • On the top right, click on "Rating" to select the order in which you want to see the chart.

    • From lowest ratings to highest ratings or the other way around.

    • Enjoy!
